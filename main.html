Roses are one of the most popular and loved flowers worldwide. They grow as shrubs, climbers, or trailing plants. They have fragrant petals that come in a wide variety of colors (red, white, yellow, pink, orange, even multicolored). Most roses have thorns (prickles) on their stems for protection.
